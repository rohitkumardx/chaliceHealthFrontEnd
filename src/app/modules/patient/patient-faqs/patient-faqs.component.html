Telehealth Appointments

A telehealth appointment allows patients to consult with healthcare providers remotely, providing medical care without the need for in-person visits.

Patients can easily book a telehealth appointment through the Chalice Health platform by selecting their preferred provider and scheduling a time. At the scheduled appointment, they can join the secure virtual session to receive care from the comfort of home.
