About Chalice Health

Chalice Health is a patient-centered healthcare platform that makes finding the care you need easier than ever. We connect you with a network of qualified and compassionate providers offering a variety of services, including:
Virtual consultations: Get expert advice from the comfort of your home.
In-person visits: Schedule appointments quickly and easily.
Home care services: Receive the care you need in the comfort of your own home.
About Insurance: Chalice health doesn't accept insurance.

FAQs

Chalice Health is your gateway to convenient and affordable healthcare access. Our platform connects you with a wide range of medical professionals, ensuring seamless assistance with your healthcare needs, regardless of your location. We prioritize convenience and affordability, offering solutions that reduce wait times and support your health journey. Whether you prefer virtual consultations or in-person visits, we provide options tailored to your needs and the availability of our providers.

Chalice Health is for everyone! We cater to individuals and families, regardless of their insurance status. Our platform is particularly beneficial for those who:
  • Lack health insurance.
  • Have high-deductible health plans (HDHPs) with high out-of-pocket costs.
  • Face challenges accessing quality healthcare due to geographic location or socioeconomic factors.

Chalice Health is committed to making quality healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone. Here’s what sets us apart:
  • Discounted Rates: Enjoy significant cost savings with our transparent pricing structure. You’ll see upfront pricing, up to 60% lower than traditional healthcare costs.
  • No Hidden Fees: We prioritize transparency. The price you see is the price you pay – no surprise charges or confusing billing practices.
  • No Insurance Required: We believe access to care shouldn’t be limited by insurance status. Utilize our platform regardless of your insurance coverage.

No, Chalice Health is not a substitute for traditional healthcare providers. We work collaboratively to complement existing healthcare services. Our platform offers a convenient and accessible way to connect with qualified professionals for consultations, prescriptions (when appropriate), and ongoing care management.
